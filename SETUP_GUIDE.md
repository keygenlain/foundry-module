# Spell and Steel - Quick Setup Guide

## Installation Steps

1. **Download the Module**
   - Get the `spell-and-steel` folder

2. **Place in Foundry Systems**
   - Windows: `%APPDATA%\FoundryVTT\Data\systems\spell-and-steel\`
   - Mac: `~/Library/Application Support/FoundryVTT/Data/systems/spell-and-steel/`
   - Linux: `~/.config/FoundryVTT/Data/systems/spell-and-steel/`

3. **Restart Foundry VTT**
   - Close and reopen the application

4. **Create a World**
   - Select "Spell and Steel" as the system

### For Players

#### Option 1: Character Builder Wizard (Recommended for New Players)

1. **Create Character** - Click "Create Actor" â†’ Type "Character"
2. **Open the Wizard** - Click the "ðŸ“‹ Character Builder" button on the character sheet
3. **Follow the Steps**:
   - **Step 1**: Enter your name and choose ancestry
   - **Step 2**: Distribute 10 attribute points (Vigor, Agility, Smarts, Spirit)
   - **Step 3**: Distribute 10 skill points among 23 skills
   - **Step 4**: Choose weapons, armor, and 2 starting spells (250 Silver budget)
   - **Step 5**: Review and click "Create Character"
4. **Done!** Your character is ready to adventure

#### Option 2: Manual Character Sheet (Advanced Players)

1. **Create Character** - Click "Create Actor" â†’ Type "Character"

2. **Name Your Character**
   - Fill in name and ancestry (Dwarf, Elf, Human, Orc, Ratfolk)

3. **Distribute Attributes** (10 points total)
   - Vigor (Strength/Endurance)
   - Agility (Dexterity/Reflexes)
   - Smarts (Intelligence/Wits)
   - Spirit (Willpower/Charisma)

4. **Pick Starting Spells** (2 spells)
   - Browse the Spell List in the Spells tab
   - Create spell items and add to character

5. **Distribute Skills** (10 points total)
   - Pick your character's strengths
   - Divide among 23 available skills
   - Each skill tied to an attribute

6. **Add Equipment** (250 Silver to spend)
   - Add weapons and armor
   - Create items via the Inventory tab

## Common Actions

### Rolling Dice Pools

**For Skills:**
1. Go to Skills tab
2. Click "Roll" next to any skill
3. Results show in chat with success count

**For Attacks:**
1. Go to Inventory tab â†’ Weapons
2. Click on a weapon to attack
3. Chat displays hits vs Success Threshold 3

**For Spells:**
1. Go to Spells tab
2. Click "Cast" on a spell
3. System deducts mana automatically
4. Roll appears in chat

### Managing Health & Mana

- Drag sliders or type in values in Attributes tab
- Health = Vigor Ã— 5
- Mana = Spirit Ã— 5
- Click "Rest 8 Hours" to fully recover

### Using Focus Tokens

1. Award tokens from Attributes tab (GM can do this)
2. Player uses token to:
   - Reroll any dice pool result
   - Add 1 die to a pool

### Creating Items

1. Go to relevant tab (Inventory for weapons/armor, Spells for spells)
2. Click "+ Add [Type]"
3. Fill in name, damage/manacost, price, etc.
4. Right-click item to edit details

## Tips & Tricks

### For Effective Roleplay
- Use the Biography tab to track character motivations
- Follow the Focus Token system for great moments
- Award Focus Tokens for unexpected solutions
- Players can use tokens to turn the tide in crucial moments

### Combat Tips
- Calculate dice pools: Skill Level + Attribute
- Success = 4 or higher on d6
- Exploding 6s means rolling extra dice
- Opposed rolls: whoever has more successes wins

### Spell Management
- Learn spells equal to (Skill + Spirit) Ã— 1.5 rounded up
- Can only cast spells up to your skill tier (1-3)
- Magic damage ignores armor
- Mana recovers with 8-hour rest

### Ancestry Bonuses
- **Dwarf**: +1 Vigor, immune to knockback
- **Ratfolk**: +1 Agility, once/session turn failed roll into success
- **Human**: +1 to any skill, combat attribute boost
- **Orc**: +1 Vigor + Brawl, inspire allies
- **Elf**: +1 Smarts + Ranged, +2 dice on Lore

## Troubleshooting

### Dice won't roll
- Check that actor is selected
- Verify skill exists in character data
- Try refreshing the page

### Spells not casting
- Check mana available (shows in Vitals)
- Verify spell tier matches your skill level
- Make sure spell is owned by character

### Items not appearing
- Click "Create Item" to make new items
- Verify item type (weapon, spell, armor, etc.)
- Check that item is assigned to correct actor

### Chat messages not showing
- Verify chat is accessible in the sidebar
- Check browser console for errors
- Try refreshing the page
